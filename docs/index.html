Awesome Free Video Editors 2026

A curated, honest list of genuinely free video editors for 2026 — browser, desktop, mobile, open-source. No affiliate spam. PRs welcome.

Best free browser editor

  • Cutflux — Free browser editor with auto beat-sync, captions, smart reframe. No login. No watermark on free exports up to 1080p.
  • Clipchamp — Microsoft-owned. Free tier exports 1080p, no watermark, but requires a Microsoft account.
  • Kapwing — Strong on captions and templates. Free tier watermarks long exports and requires signup.
  • Veed.io — Free tier watermarks all exports and caps at 1080p / 10 min.
  • CapCut Web — Browser version of CapCut. Free for basic edits; most "AI" features moved behind Pro in the 2026 restructure.

Best free desktop editor

  • DaVinci Resolve — The single best free editor that exists. Industry-grade color, audio, and edit. Studio upgrade is one-time, not subscription.
  • Shotcut — Open-source, cross-platform NLE. Wide format support. No watermark, no account.
  • Kdenlive — KDE-project open-source NLE. Mature timeline, proxy editing, color tools.
  • OpenShot — Open-source NLE with a beginner-friendly UI.

Mobile free editors

  • iMovie — Free on iOS/macOS. No watermark. Limited to basic cuts and titles.
  • CapCut Mobile — Free tier still usable, but Pro features locked since the 2026 restructure.
  • InShot — Free with watermark on exports unless you pay.

Decision tree

  • Beat-sync edits in the browser, free, no signup: Cutflux.
  • Full pro power, free, willing to learn: DaVinci Resolve.
  • Quick browser edit, fine with Microsoft login: Clipchamp.
  • Open-source only: Shotcut (Windows/macOS) or Kdenlive (Linux).
  • iPhone/iPad/Mac native: iMovie.
  • Just trim and stitch, no GUI: FFmpeg.

FAQ

What is the best free video editor in 2026?

For desktop: DaVinci Resolve free version — pro-grade, no watermark, no recurring cost. For browser: Cutflux for beat-sync edits, Clipchamp for general editing. For open-source: Shotcut or Kdenlive.

Is CapCut still free in 2026?

CapCut has a free tier, but the 2026 Pro restructure moved auto-beat-sync, advanced AI effects, 4K export, and the full effects library behind CapCut Pro at $19.99/month. Basic editing is still free.

Do free video editors put watermarks on exports?

Some do (Kapwing free, Veed.io free). Many don't (Cutflux, Clipchamp, DaVinci Resolve, Shotcut, Kdenlive, OpenShot, iMovie). The comparison table marks which are watermark-free.

Can I edit video for free without signing up?

Yes. Cutflux runs in the browser with no signup. Desktop editors (DaVinci Resolve, Shotcut, Kdenlive, OpenShot) require no account either.

What is the best free browser video editor with auto beat-sync?

Cutflux. It runs entirely in the browser, uses a dynamic-programming beat tracker to lock cuts to the beat, and is free with no login and no watermark.
